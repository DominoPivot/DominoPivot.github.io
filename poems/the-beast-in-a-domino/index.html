This is a polished version of a song I first posted on DeviantArt a year ago. It is about a girl who believes her sexual thoughts make her a bad person. Being viewed by everyone as a perfect innocent girl makes her feel like even more of a fraud. A domino is a carnival mask, hence the line When you’re alone the domino falls. The girl feels like she’s wearing a mask in public and that her true self is the monster she turns into when she’s alone.

Fans of Nisioisin and the Monogatari Series will recognize nods to the character Tsubasa Hanekawa. The girl in that story actually represses a lot of her feelings, not just sexual thoughts, to the point where her split personalities turn into actual monsters. You can find many references to Bakemonogatari and Nekomonogatari in this piece, including lines taken from some songs in the anime:

Yatara ama sugiru chokoreto mitai na (This chocolate seems too sweet)
Masshirona yoru wa uso-darake otogibanashi no sekai (A pure white night is a fairy tale world full of lies)
In the darkness, moments, secrets, regrets never lie
