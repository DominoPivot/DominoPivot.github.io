Noticing a lot of my songs were more bitter than bittersweet, I decided to take inspiration from Aurelio Voltaire, who writes humorous songs about horrible things. There are obvious nods to Death Death (Devil, Devil, Evil, Evil, Song) in this composition, both in the themes and structure. The bit where the narrator looses their cool is also a nod to Voltaire’s Oh Lord (Wake the Dead), which ends in an improvised rant about God. But in the end, I guess this still isn’t a happy song. Ooops!

The first three lines are a reference to my previous song The Beast in a Domino which told a similar story from a different perspective. I hope the bold sexual themes of this song aren’t too much for my free hosting service or the eyes of my readers.
